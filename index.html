About Us

Built for Precision.
Driven by Trust.

"Sarva Vahana" — derived from Sanskrit, meaning "Every Vehicle, Every Journey." It embodies our commitment to providing the right vehicle for every need, ensuring every journey is safe, timely, and seamless.

With over 5 years of experience in ground transportation, Sarvavahana Logistics is a Hyderabad-based company purpose-built for the aviation and corporate sector. Founded by Rahul Reddy Keshampally, we understand that airline crew and corporate professionals operate on time-critical schedules. A delayed cab isn't just an inconvenience — it disrupts flight operations and business commitments.

Our partnership with IndiGo and leading MNCs reflects the trust that industry leaders place in our years of operational excellence, safety standards, and unwavering commitment to reliability.
